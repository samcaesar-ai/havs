About

"The best deals in Nordic timber aren't made across a desk. They're made across twenty years of trust."

David Hav has spent his career in the Nordic timber and construction industry building long-term relationships that move products from catalogue to specification. HAVS is the practice he runs from Denmark, working across Sweden, Denmark, and Norway.

With a focus on CLT, glulam, and prefabricated timber systems, David brings both technical depth and a genuine enthusiasm for the material — and the people who work with it.
