Materials

The materials.

HAVS works with the structural timber products that define modern Nordic construction — CLT, glulam, and prefabricated systems from Scandinavian producers.

Structural

Cross-Laminated
Timber

CLT is the defining material of contemporary mass timber construction — solid panels built from alternating layers of structural-grade timber, with the grain running perpendicular in each layer.

The result is a rigid, stable panel with excellent load-bearing capacity in two directions. CLT panels are used for floors, walls, and roofs in residential, commercial, and public buildings across Scandinavia.

SpecificationRange
Layers3, 5, 7, or 9 — odd number, symmetric
Panel thickness60–500 mm depending on layer count
Standard widths600–3000 mm
SpeciesNorway spruce (primary); Scots pine, Douglas fir
Strength classC24, C16 — EN 338

Structural

Glued Laminated
Timber

Glulam is the workhorse of Nordic timber structures — engineered beams built from horizontal layers of structural timber bonded with structural adhesive, producing members of exceptional strength and dimensional precision.

Glulam beams can be produced straight or curved, at sizes that far exceed what is possible in solid timber, and with consistent, predictable structural performance. They are used in everything from residential roofs to large-span public buildings.

SpecificationRange
Strength classGL 24h, GL 28h, GL 32h — EN 14080
Cross-sectionsCustom — up to 1800 mm depth
LengthUp to 30 m standard; longer by arrangement
SpeciesNorway spruce primary; Nordic pine, larch
SurfaceIndustrial, structural, or architectural finish

Prefabricated

Prefabricated
Timber Systems

Prefabricated timber elements — wall panels, floor cassettes, and roof elements — are manufactured to precise tolerances in a controlled factory environment, then delivered to site ready for immediate assembly.

The advantages over site-built systems are measurable: reduced on-site labour, faster build programmes, better quality control, and significantly lower construction waste. For CLT-frame or hybrid structures, prefabrication is increasingly the default approach.

System typeApplication
Wall panelsLoad-bearing or non-structural; insulated, pre-clad
Floor cassettesTimber-frame deck; span up to 8 m standard
Roof elementsFlat and pitched; integrated waterproofing available
Hybrid systemsTimber + steel or concrete combined elements
