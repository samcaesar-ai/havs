HAVS works in the space between product and project. We introduce timber manufacturers to architects who specify, contractors who build, and developers who commission.

The result is fewer cold meetings, more qualified opportunities, and timber products that end up in the right buildings.

We don't operate as a traditional sales agency. We operate as a long-term partner to the producers we represent — and as a trusted, technically fluent voice to the construction professionals we work with.

This means that when David picks up the phone or walks into an architect's office, the relationship is already warm. The products he represents have context. The conversations start at a different level.

Fewer cold meetings.

We open doors based on technical relevance and mutual value — not volume. A meeting arranged by HAVS is a meeting worth having: both sides prepared, both sides interested.

More right buildings.

Our goal is to see timber products implemented in the projects where they make the greatest difference. That means being selective about introductions, and honest about fit.

Services

What working with HAVS looks like.

For manufacturers

Market development

Opening new relationships with architects, contractors, and developers across Denmark, Sweden, and Norway. Long-term pipeline building, not short-term lead generation.

For manufacturers

Specification support

Working alongside design teams during early project phases to get timber products into specifications — with the technical fluency to make the case properly.

For architects & contractors

Supplier introductions

Direct connections to Nordic timber producers with the commercial and technical context already established. No cold calls, no introductory presentations from scratch.

For all

Tender & project advice

Guidance through the supply chain at the moments that matter — tender stage, value engineering, material substitution — with someone who knows the market.
