About David

"Timber is a technical sale. But it's also a people business. The best relationships in this industry last decades — and that's exactly what I'm building."

David Hav, København

HAVS

David Hav

David has spent his career in the Nordic timber and construction industry — building the long-term relationships that move products from catalogue to specification, and from specification to the buildings that define Scandinavian cities.

HAVS is the practice he runs from Copenhagen, working across Sweden, Denmark, and Norway. It is deliberately small: one person, a clear focus, and twenty years of trust built up on both sides of the table.

Before founding HAVS, David held roles with timber producers and construction industry organisations across the region. He knows the industry from the sawmill floor to the architect's desk — and that breadth is what makes the introductions he makes land differently.

He speaks Danish, Swedish, and English, and works comfortably across the regulatory and commercial cultures of all three Scandinavian markets.
