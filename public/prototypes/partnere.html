Partners

The producers.

HAVS represents a select group of Scandinavian timber producers — chosen for the quality of their product, the depth of their technical capability, and the fit with the markets we work in.

Featured partner

Södra

Södra is one of Sweden's largest and most technically advanced timber producers — a cooperative owned by 52,000 forest owners, with manufacturing facilities across southern Sweden.

HAVS represents Södra across the Danish and Norwegian construction markets, introducing their CLT, glulam, and sawn timber products to architects, contractors, and developers with the relevant projects and specifications.

Södra's CLT and glulam products are manufactured at Södra Wood Värö and Krylbo, with delivery capacity across Scandinavia and northern Europe.
